About this library

This library brings together supporting materials planned for the implementation of the AccessOps Framework: Continuous Digital Accessibility. Its purpose is to organize, in a single space, guides, checklists, templates, examples and operational documents that help teams apply the methodology in a gradual, adaptable and documented way.

At this stage, the materials are marked as under construction. This status is intentional: the library should work both as an overview of the planned document set and as a living checklist of what may still be developed, reviewed, prioritized, translated or reorganized over time.

The materials in this library are methodological support instruments. They do not replace WCAG, applicable technical standards, specialized evaluations, testing with persons with disabilities or legal responsibilities.

How to use this library

The library was designed to support organizations at different maturity levels in digital accessibility governance. A team may begin with the introductory guides, move on to the operational checklists, adapt the record templates and, gradually, structure routines for monitoring, prioritization, automation and reassessment.

The order of the materials does not need to be followed rigidly. AccessOps is based on the idea of a continuous cycle, so each organization can begin at the most urgent point for its operation, such as diagnosis, correction, monitoring, governance, documentation, maturity or automation.
